Introduction

Pets are more than just animals; they are companions that bring joy and comfort to our lives. This article explores the various types of pets people keep and their significance in different cultures around the world.

Why People Keep Pets

People keep pets for various reasons, including companionship, emotional support, and even health benefits. Pets can help reduce stress, encourage physical activity, and provide a sense of responsibility. For many, pets become beloved members of the family, offering unconditional love and loyalty.
